About the club

We help students develop real‑world speaking skills, journalistic integrity, and the confidence to present news in front of large audiences. Through teamwork, creativity, and leadership, our members become the voice of the school.

Public speaking

Weekly speaking drills, anchoring, and stage presence training.

Journalism

Report writing, interviewing, and editing school news.

Teamwork

Collaborative projects, from news casting to event coverage.

Leadership

Take charge as editors, anchors, and club officers.

Our activities

News casting

Live morning announcements and assembly news segments.

Interviews

Interview teachers, students, and special guests.

School reporting

Write articles for the school blog and monthly magazine.

Event coverage

Media coverage of sports day, cultural fests, and more.

Debate & speech

Practice impromptu speaking and structured debates.
